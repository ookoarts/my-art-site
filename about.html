My Story

Lawrence Ooko Wasonga(b."97) is a Nairobi-based contemporary artist, born and partly raised in Homa Bay, Kenya. Ooko commands a dynamic range of styles, working primarily in acrylics on canvas while dabbling in pastels and oils.

A free spirit and superb storyteller, he tells his tales most vividly through painting.

I view art as the highest expression of human intelligence, an invitation for audiences to travel, feel, learn, and question societal norms.


His work transcends borders and cultural boundaries, exploring themes from everyday food traditions to civil rights movements, activism, and portraiture that captures the essence of individuals and communities.
